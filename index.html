Trust architecture

Build Stashd as a compliance-first teen finance platform.

The investor story gets stronger when the regulated parts are clearly partnered, disclosed, and de-risked.

FDIC-insured cash

Do: Use a sponsor bank / neobank partner for cash deposits and debit card issuance.

Avoid: Do not imply stablecoins themselves are FDIC insured.

Stablecoin rewards

Do: Frame as partner-powered rewards/cash-back or approved growth bucket.

Avoid: Do not promise yield directly from Stashd without securities and money-transmission review.

Minors + privacy

Do: Parent consent, custodial structure, COPPA-safe data model, restricted teen permissions.

Avoid: Teen social/referral mechanics that leak personal data or encourage risky behavior.

Trust-first positioning

The product is not “crypto for kids.”

Stashd is a teen money platform that uses stablecoin infrastructure behind the scenes where it creates better rewards, lower costs, and programmable money movement. Teens experience goals, chores, limits, rewards, and financial confidence — not wallets, seed phrases, or speculation.
